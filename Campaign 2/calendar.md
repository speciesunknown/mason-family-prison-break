# Calendar

The year is 360 days long, divided into four seasons of 90 days each.

| Season | Days | Months |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Winter** | 1–90 | Frostmere (1–30), Deepcold (31–60), Thawbreak (61–90) |
| **Spring** | 91–180 | Mudwake (91–120), Bloomrise (121–150), Greenfield (151–180) |
| **Summer** | 181–270 | Longlight (181–210), Highsun (211–240), Goldtide (241–270) |
| **Autumn** | 271–360 | Harvestfall (271–300), Dimleaf (301–330), Greymarch (331–360) |

Each month is 30 days. Weeks are 6 days long (5 per month).
