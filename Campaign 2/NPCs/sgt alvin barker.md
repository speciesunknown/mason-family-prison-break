sgt, 4th dragoons

Captured by the resistance. Due to noble background, Duzak plans to ransom him off

Shadow then leaked the information about his succession rank (admittedly it's only 17th) which promptly caused barker to threaten to leak this info. They agreed shadow would pay him 8000GP next time they meet.

was eventually ransomed off back to his family. Hopes to eventually meet the party again on the road
