Mr Fuckles the Cabbagineer

**About Mr Fuckles:**
- Expert in cabbage engineering and cultivation
- Collects spoons obsessively - claims to have over 1,000 unique spoons
- Each spoon has a story, and he remembers every single one
- Uses different spoons for different types of cabbage preparation
- Believes that the right spoon is crucial for proper cabbage alchemy

**The Many Spoons:**
- **The Cabbage Cutter:** A serrated silver spoon used exclusively for slicing cabbage leaves
- **The Stirring Spoon of Destiny:** A wooden spoon that's been passed down through 7 generations of cabbage engineers
- **The Ladle of Lamentation:** A large copper spoon used for extracting cabbage juice - Mr Fuckles claims it weeps when used incorrectly
- **The Teaspoon of Truth:** A tiny ornate spoon that can apparently detect if cabbage has been improperly stored
- **The Serving Spoon of Serenity:** A massive ceremonial spoon used only for the most important cabbage presentations
- **The Spork of Suspicion:** A hybrid utensil that Mr Fuckles keeps locked away - he's not sure if it counts as a spoon or not
- **The Spoon Collection:** Organized by material, size, purpose, and "cabbage compatibility rating"
- Refuses to use forks - considers them "cabbage's natural enemy"
- Has been known to challenge people to spoon duels over cabbage-related disputes

**Stats:**
- STR: 10
- DEX: 14
- CON: 12
- INT: 16
- WIS: 15
- CHA: 13

**Skills:**
- Nature: +6 (expertise in cabbage cultivation)
- Investigation: +6 (analyzing cabbage quality)
- Sleight of Hand: +5 (precise spoon manipulation)
- History: +5 (knowledge of spoon traditions)
- Persuasion: +4 (convincing others about cabbage importance)

**Abilities:**
- **Cabbage Expertise:** Can identify any type of cabbage, its origin, quality, and optimal preparation method
- **Spoon Mastery:** Proficient with all spoon types; can use spoons as improvised weapons (1d4 bludgeoning)
- **Cabbage Alchemy:** Can create various cabbage-based concoctions with the right materials and spoons
- **Spoon Recall:** Remembers every spoon in his collection and its history in perfect detail
