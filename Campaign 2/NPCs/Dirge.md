Dirge

Klevane's former mentor. The one who sent him to ambush the party. A half-elf who looks nothing like one — built like a siege engine, with a voice that makes people want to follow him off a cliff.

**Appears in:** [M08 The Ambuscade](../Missions/M08%20The%20Ambuscade.md)

**Race:** Half-elf
**Role:** Antagonist, Klevane's former mentor

**Stats:**

20 12 16 10 12 16   136HP (16d8+64)  AC18 (elven plate)  SPD30

Saves: STR +9, CON +8, CHA +7
Skills: Athletics +9, Intimidation +10, Persuasion +10
Senses: Darkvision 60ft, Passive Perception 11
Languages: Common, Elvish
CR: 9

Fey Ancestry — advantage on saves against being charmed, magic can't put him to sleep
Reckless Attack — at the start of his turn, can gain advantage on all melee attacks that turn, but attacks against him also have advantage until his next turn

**Rage (3/day)**
Bonus action. Lasts 1 minute. While raging:
- +3 bonus to melee damage
- Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage
- Advantage on STR checks and STR saves
- Cannot be charmed or frightened

Actions

Multiattack — Dirge makes two greatsword attacks

Greatsword x2  +9, 2D6+5 slashing (+3 while raging)

Commanding Shout (recharge 5-6)
    Each creature of Dirge's choice within 30 feet must make a DC15 WIS save or be frightened for 1 minute. A frightened creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.

Bonus Actions

Rage (see above)

Reactions

Retaliate — when a creature within 5 feet deals damage to Dirge, he can make one greatsword attack against that creature

**Personality:**
- Commands loyalty through sheer force of personality rather than fear
- Speaks plainly, never raises his voice — doesn't need to
- Believes completely in whatever cause he serves; there's no doubt in him
- Treats those beneath him with a kind of brutal paternalism — he'll break your legs and tell you it's for your own good

**Relationship with Klevane:**
- Took Klevane under his wing, trained him, gave him purpose
- Klevane looked up to him as a father figure
- Since returning from the underworld, Klevane sees Dirge clearly for the first time — a manipulator who used loyalty as a leash
- Dirge will see Klevane's betrayal as personal. Not angry. Disappointed. Which is worse.

**The Gang:**
- [Needle](Needle.md) — Wood elf scout. Dirge's forward lookout and tracker. Barely speaks, never misses.
- [Vael Crookedbow](Vael%20Crookedbow.md) — Half-elf lieutenant. Keeps the rank-and-file in line through fear. The only one making tactical calls when Dirge is raging.
- [Thornwick](Thornwick.md) — Wood elf druid. Camp healer and the closest thing Dirge has to an advisor. The crack in the gang's loyalty.

**Prisoners:**
- [Maren Soldoval](Maren%20Soldoval.md) — Half-elf merchant captured during a caravan raid. Kept alive because Thornwick saw value in her.
- [Old Ferris](Old%20Ferris.md) — Human farmer taken as leverage against a nearby settlement. Knows the camp routine cold.
