Maren Soldoval

A half-elf merchant who was hauling alchemical supplies between towns when [Dirge](Dirge.md)'s gang hit her caravan. Three guards dead, cargo seized, Maren kept alive because [Thornwick](Thornwick.md) recognised the value of the supplies and figured the merchant who sourced them was worth more than a quick ransom.

**Race:** Half-elf
**Role:** Prisoner, potential ally
**Affiliation:** Independent trader (captured by [Dirge](Dirge.md)'s gang)

**Stats:**

8 12 10 16 14 14   22HP (5d8)  AC11  SPD30

Skills: Persuasion +6, Insight +4, Arcana +5, Investigation +5
Senses: Darkvision 60ft, Passive Perception 12
Languages: Common, Elvish, Dwarvish, Gnomish

**What she knows:**
- The gang's camp layout — she's been moved twice and has a good sense of the perimeter
- [Thornwick](Thornwick.md) has been keeping her fed and relatively unharmed; she suspects he has his own agenda
- [Vael](Vael%20Crookedbow.md) wanted to kill her after the first week. Dirge overruled it.
- She's heard the gang discussing an "ambush" and knows the general direction they expect the targets from

**Personality:**
- Pragmatic and sharp; already working on ways to leverage her situation
- Not a fighter but not a coward — she'll act if given an opening
- Resentful but controlled; she won't do anything stupid out of spite
- If rescued, she has trade contacts that could be useful to the party down the line
