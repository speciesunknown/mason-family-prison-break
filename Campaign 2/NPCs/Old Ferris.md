Old Ferris

A human farmer taken from a smallholding near the forest edge. [Dirge](Dirge.md)'s gang grabbed him as leverage to keep the nearby settlement from reporting their movements. He's been a prisoner for weeks. Thin, bruised, but stubbornly alive. The gang underestimates him because he's old and quiet, but Ferris has been paying attention.

**Race:** Human
**Role:** Prisoner, potential informant
**Affiliation:** None (captured by [Dirge](Dirge.md)'s gang)

**Stats:**

12 10 13 10 15 11   16HP (3d8+3)  AC10  SPD30

Skills: Animal Handling +4, Perception +4, Survival +4
Senses: Passive Perception 14
Languages: Common

**What he knows:**
- The daily routine of the camp — guard rotations, meal times, when [Dirge](Dirge.md) sleeps
- [Needle](Needle.md) leaves camp before dawn every day and doesn't return until midday; he's the one to worry about in the woods
- There's tension between [Vael](Vael%20Crookedbow.md) and [Thornwick](Thornwick.md) — Vael thinks the old druid is going soft, Thornwick thinks Vael is going to get everyone killed
- Dirge has been expecting someone — possibly Klevane — to report back. He's been getting impatient.

**Personality:**
- Weathered, patient, observant — the kind of quiet that comes from a life spent watching crops grow
- Doesn't trust easily but responds to genuine kindness
- Angry underneath the calm; he wants to go home and he wants the people who took him to pay for it
- Will share everything he knows freely if rescued, no bargaining needed
