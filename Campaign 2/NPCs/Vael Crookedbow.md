Vael Crookedbow

[Dirge](Dirge.md)'s second-in-command. A half-elf with a badly-set broken nose and a reputation for creative cruelty. Where Dirge leads through presence, Vael keeps the rank-and-file in line through fear of what she'll do if they step out. She earned the name "Crookedbow" after she beat a deserter half to death with his own weapon.

**Race:** Half-elf
**Role:** Lieutenant, enforcer
**Affiliation:** [Dirge](Dirge.md)'s gang

**Stats:**

16 16 14 13 10 14   85HP (13d8+26)  AC17 (half plate)  SPD30

Saves: STR +6, DEX +6
Skills: Athletics +6, Intimidation +7, Deception +5, Perception +3
Senses: Darkvision 60ft, Passive Perception 13
Languages: Common, Elvish, Thieves' Cant
CR: 5

Fey Ancestry — advantage on saves against being charmed, magic can't put him to sleep
Pack Tactics — has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of Vael's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated

Actions

Multiattack — Vael makes two scimitar attacks or one scimitar attack and uses Bark an Order

Scimitar x2  +6, 1D6+3 slashing
Hand crossbow  +6, 1D6+3 piercing (30/120)

Bark an Order
    One ally within 30 feet who can hear Vael can immediately use their reaction to make one weapon attack or move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

Bonus Actions

Dirty Fighting — after hitting a creature with a melee attack, Vael can attempt to shove them prone (target makes DC14 STR save)

Reactions

Redirect Attack — when a creature Vael can see targets her with an attack, she can choose an ally within 5 feet. The attack targets that ally instead. She uses this without hesitation.

**Personality:**
- Sharp tongue, mean streak, zero patience for excuses
- Genuinely respects Dirge — he's the only person she's ever met who she couldn't intimidate
- Treats the gang like tools. Useful tools get maintained; broken tools get thrown away.
- Has a pragmatic survivalist streak — she'll retreat if the fight turns, and she'll sell out anyone except Dirge to save her own skin

**Role in the Ambush:**
- Commands the main body of bandits in the kill zone
- Keeps the ambushers disciplined and in position until the signal
- If things go sideways, she's the one making tactical calls on the ground while Dirge fights
