Morrigan

A blue half-dragon with full wings. Tall, scaled, imposing — and apparently unarmed. Doesn't need weapons. Her claws, tail, and breath are more than enough.

**Race:** Half-dragon (blue)
**Role:** Combatant

**Stats:**

18 14 16 12 10 14   119HP (14d8+56)  AC17 (natural armor)  SPD30, Fly 60

Saves: STR +8, CON +7
Skills: Athletics +8, Intimidation +6, Perception +4, Acrobatics +6
Senses: Blindsight 10ft, Darkvision 60ft, Passive Perception 14
Languages: Common, Draconic
CR: 7

Damage Resistance: Lightning
Dragon Wings — Morrigan has a flying speed of 60ft
Unarmed Menace — Morrigan's natural weapons count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance

Actions

Multiattack — Morrigan makes three attacks: one bite and two claw attacks. She can substitute one claw attack for a tail sweep.

Bite  +8, 1D10+4 piercing + 1D6 lightning
Claw x2  +8, 1D8+4 slashing
Tail Sweep  +8, 1D8+4 bludgeoning, target must make DC16 STR save or be knocked prone

Lightning Breath (recharge 5-6)
    Morrigan exhales a 30-foot line of lightning, 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a DC15 DEX save, taking 6D10 lightning damage on a failed save, or half on a success.

Bonus Actions

Wingbeat — Morrigan beats her wings. Each creature within 10 feet must succeed on a DC16 STR save or be pushed 10 feet away and knocked prone. Morrigan can then fly up to half her flying speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

Reactions

Tail Lash — when a creature moves within 5 feet of Morrigan, she can make one tail sweep attack against them
