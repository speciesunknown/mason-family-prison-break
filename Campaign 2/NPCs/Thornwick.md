Thornwick

The gang's healer and the closest thing [Dirge](Dirge.md) has to an advisor. An older wood elf who was cast out of his circle decades ago for reasons he won't discuss. Walks with a limp, carries a gnarled staff wrapped in dried sinew. Keeps the gang alive — patches wounds, purifies water, wards the camp. Quiet authority that even [Vael](Vael%20Crookedbow.md) doesn't challenge.

**Race:** Wood elf
**Role:** Healer, advisor
**Affiliation:** [Dirge](Dirge.md)'s gang

**Stats:**

10 14 14 13 18 10   55HP (10d8+10)  AC14 (hide armor + DEX)  SPD35

Saves: INT +4, WIS +7
Skills: Medicine +7, Nature +4, Perception +7, Survival +7
Senses: Darkvision 60ft, Passive Perception 17
Languages: Common, Elvish, Druidic, Sylvan
CR: 4

Fey Ancestry — advantage on saves against being charmed, magic can't put him to sleep
Mask of the Wild — can attempt to hide when only lightly obscured by foliage, rain, snow, mist, or similar

**Spellcasting** (Druid, WIS, spell save DC15, +7 to hit)

Cantrips: Produce Flame, Thorn Whip, Druidcraft
1st (4 slots): Entangle, Healing Word, Faerie Fire, Goodberry
2nd (3 slots): Spike Growth, Barkskin, Hold Person
3rd (2 slots): Plant Growth, Dispel Magic

Actions

Thorn Whip  +7, 1D6 piercing, 30ft range, pulls target 10ft closer on hit
Produce Flame  +7, 2D8 fire, 30ft range
Quarterstaff  +3, 1D6 bludgeoning (versatile 1D8)

**Personality:**
- Speaks in measured, careful sentences — weighs every word
- Not evil, but long past caring about conventional morality; survival is the only creed he follows now
- Feels a paternal obligation to the younger gang members; quietly resents that Dirge burns through them
- The only person in the gang who will openly disagree with Dirge, and the only one Dirge allows to

**Role in the Ambush:**
- Positioned in the treeline behind the main force
- Uses Entangle and Spike Growth to control the battlefield and cut off escape routes
- Keeps the gang fighting with Healing Word
- If the party tries to parley, Thornwick might actually listen — he's the crack in the gang's loyalty
