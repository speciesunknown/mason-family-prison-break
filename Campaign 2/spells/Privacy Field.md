# Privacy Field

Level 5 spell

Duration: up to 1 minute

Range: Self

When you cast the spell, you and any creatures you choose within 120 feet may have a conversation lasting for up to 24 hours. During that time, 1 minute will pass in the outside world. While within the radius of effect, only creatures chosen when the spell is cast may hear your conversation, either through their own sense, or by spells such as scry.
