# Weather Tables

Roll **d10** each day. Most results (4–7) give typical seasonal weather. Extremes are rare.

---

### Winter

| d10 | Weather |
|-----|---------|
| 1 | Blizzard — whiteout, travel near-impossible. Roll a random encounter. |
| 2–3 | Heavy snow — thick snowfall, reduced visibility, travel slowed |
| 4–5 | Overcast and cold — grey sky, biting wind, dry cold |
| 6–7 | Light snow — thin flurries, dusting on the ground, manageable |
| 8–9 | Clear and freezing — bright skies, cold but calm, good visibility |
| 10 | Crisp and beautiful — sun on fresh snow, no wind, perfect winter day |

---

### Spring

| d10 | Weather |
|-----|---------|
| 1 | Thunderstorms — violent thunder, lightning, flash flooding. Roll a random encounter. |
| 2–3 | Heavy rain — steady downpour, roads turn to mud, rivers rising |
| 4–5 | Scattered showers — bursts of rain between sunshine |
| 6–7 | Overcast and mild — cloudy but comfortable, damp air |
| 8–9 | Warm and breezy — pleasant wind, partly cloudy, good for travel |
| 10 | Glorious — perfect warmth, gentle breeze, not a cloud in sight |

---

### Summer

| d10 | Weather |
|-----|---------|
| 1 | Scorching heat — dangerously hot, heatstroke risk. Roll a random encounter. |
| 2–3 | Heavy thunderstorm — torrential rain, hail possible, lightning |
| 4–5 | Hot and still — oppressive heat, no breeze, animals seek shade |
| 6–7 | Warm and clear — classic summer day, blue skies, long daylight |
| 8–9 | Bright and breezy — warm with a pleasant wind, ideal conditions |
| 10 | Perfect day — golden light, cool breeze, everything feels easy |

---

### Autumn

| d10 | Weather |
|-----|---------|
| 1 | Gale — violent wind, branches and debris flying. Roll a random encounter. |
| 2–3 | Cold downpour — near-freezing rain, soaking through cloaks |
| 4–5 | Cool and breezy — chilly gusts, leaves blowing, smell of decay |
| 6–7 | Light rain — steady drizzle, damp chill, puddles forming |
| 8–9 | Crisp and clear — cool air, vivid blue sky, leaves turning colour |
| 10 | Golden autumn — warm sun, still air, bright foliage, beautiful |
