# Reality Complication Table

Roll d20 at end of each turn during Step 4: Rescue and Restore

| Roll | Complication |
|------|--------------|
| 1-2 | **Gravity Shift:** Gravity reverses for one character. They must make a STR save or fall upward, taking 1d6 bludgeoning damage. |
| 3-4 | **Time Loop:** Make a WIS save. On a failure, time resets to the beginning of your turn and you must repeat your turn; you, and only you, retain full knowledge of what happened before the time loop.|
| 5-6 | **Spatial Tear:** A reality tear opens near a random character. STR save or take 1d8 force damage and be pushed 10 feet. |
| 7-8 | **Magical Feedback:** The stabilization device pulses with energy. All characters within 15 feet make a CON save or lose concentration on spells. |
| 9-10 | **Flumph Swarm:** 1d4 additional flumphs phase into existence from the broken reality. |
| 11-12 | **Reality Echo:** A random character's shadow acts independently. The shadow makes a melee attack against its owner (1d4 psychic damage). |
| 13-14 | **Color Drain:** Magical colors drain from the area. All characters make a WIS save or have disadvantage on their next ability check. |
| 15-16 | **Dimensional Overlap:** Two characters swap positions. Randomly select two characters and swap their locations. |
| 17-18 | **Magical Static:** Spellcasting becomes unstable. Next spell cast has a 50% chance to fizzle or target a random creature instead. |
| 19-20 | **Reality Stabilizes:** The broken reality briefly aligns. All characters gain an additional action which must be used on their next turn.|
