# Elephant Graveyard

The nearby town of Pitt's Landing had a tragedy 50 years ago when the mayor's daughter was trampled by some elephants. She decided to cull most of the wild elephants which were endemic to the region and capture the remainig few to use as beasts of burden. They have one last elephant, in captivity.

In the town centre there is a monument to the slaying of the last wild elephant.

During the culling a pair of elephants escaped. Breeze was mortally wounded and travelled to the elephant graveyard to die, but her mate Tooth was recaptured. Breeze's spirit refuses to leave the area until she knows that her mate is safe.

The party will overhear a Socks, a tabaxi, talking to Nigel - actually Tooth, although the tabaxi doesn't know that. "Nigel" can smell Breeze on the party if they picked up the spear point or spent any time near the bones.
