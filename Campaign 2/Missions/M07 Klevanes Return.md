# Klevane's Return

Klevane died while fighting Knickers. The party must travel to the underworld to retrieve his soul.

## Act 1: Reaching the River Styx

- Find a way to access the underworld (portal, ritual, or guide)
- Arrive at the shore of the River Styx under a starless sky

## Act 2: Crossing the Styx

- A ferryman offers passage for a price (coins, memories, secrets, or a future favor)
- Anyone who touches the water must make a WIS save or lose specific memories
- Hostile souls and echoes of the dead may harass the party during the crossing

## Act 3: The Borderlands

- Ashen plains, ruined battlements, slow-moving processions of the dead
- Track down Klevane by questioning shades, following chains of souls, or bribing underworld functionaries
- Klevane is being held in a judgment hall or processing area

## Act 4: Retrieving Klevane

- Free Klevane's soul by infiltration, trial, or force
- Klevane may be changed by his time in the underworld
- Nickers or a rival yugoloth may try to claim Klevane's soul as leverage in the power struggle
- Escape back across the Styx before the underworld closes around the party
