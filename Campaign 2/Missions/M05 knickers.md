## Rescuing BoomBoom

[Flumph](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/17145-flumph)
There has been a magical accident and boomboom is trapped in a broken version of reality. If the party want to talk to him they need to first rescue him from his own stupidity.

**Step 1: Discover the Accident**
- Arrive at the tower of elder mages to find it partially phased out of reality
- The tower flickers between normal and distorted states and flumphs are floating around randomly
- Investigate to discover boomboom attempted an experimental spell combination that went wrong
- Find clues about what spell components were used and where the accident occurred

**Step 2: Access the Broken Reality**
- Locate the epicenter of the magical accident (likely boomboom's laboratory)
- Find a way to enter the broken reality - may require:
  - Dispelling protective wards that are now malfunctioning
  - Using specific magical items or spells to stabilize a portal
  - Following magical resonance trails left by the accident

**Step 3: Navigate the Distorted Realm**
- Enter a reality where physics and magic behave unpredictably
- Encounter reality glitches: gravity shifts, time loops, spatial distortions
- Solve puzzles or overcome challenges created by the broken reality
- Track down boomboom's location through the chaos

**Step 4: Rescue and Restore**
- Find boomboom trapped in a pocket dimension or reality loop
- The reality stabilization device requires all 6 base stats to be channeled simultaneously
- **Combat Challenge:** Angry flumphs swarm the area, attacking during initiative
- **Stabilization Challenge:** Each party member (or the party collectively) must make ability checks using all 6 stats:
  - **STR (Athletics):** Physically hold/manipulate heavy components, brace against reality distortions, or force mechanisms into place
  - **DEX (Acrobatics/Sleight of Hand):** Precisely align delicate components, maintain balance during reality shifts, or perform fine manipulation
  - **CON (Constitution Save):** Endure physical strain, resist exhaustion from reality shifts, or maintain focus under pressure
  - **INT (Investigation/Arcana/History):** Analyze the device's mechanisms, understand how components interact, or recall relevant knowledge about similar devices
  - **WIS (Perception/Insight/Survival):** Notice subtle changes in the device, read visual/auditory cues indicating progress, or sense when something is wrong
  - **CHA (Persuasion/Intimidation/Performance):** Coordinate team efforts, maintain morale under stress, or use force of personality to stabilize chaotic energy
- Success on all 6 checks (or majority success) stabilizes reality and frees boomboom
- Failure may require retries or cause additional complications
- See [Reality Complication Table](M05%20Reality%20Complication%20Table.md) - Roll d20 at end of each turn
