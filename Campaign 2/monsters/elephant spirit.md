Elephant Spirit

22 10 18 6 12 14   200HP  AC18  SPD40


Actions

Slam x2  +10, 1D12+6

Bone Blast, 30 foot line, recharge 5-6
    DC16 dex save, 3D6 piercing damage and half on a success
    DC16 con save, 3D6 psychic damage on a fail, half on a success

Legendary actions
Bone Morph (2 actions) - the creature morphs into the ground then reappears up to 60 feet to a point it can see within range, provided that point is within the elephant graveyard.

Trample (1 action) - the creature moves up to 15 feet in any direction. Any creature within the line must make a DC16 strength saving throw, taking 2D8 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half on a success.
