## 18th of winter

Arrived to find southport on fire. 
Saved many citizens, and assisted the temple in rescuing medical supplies from a burning warehouse
Came into conflict with an apparrent southport chapter of the People's Hand

## 19th
Slept at the inn, Shadow helped people salvage items from their houses

## 20th
Headed off to the west, looking for the weapons shipment. 

Ran into a troll which has been robbing travellers. Gave it some silver and promised to come back with more later

Found the barge beached at the bottom of a cliff, and a group of royal army soldiers at the top of the cliff have captured the sailors and killed them during interrogations

The party wiped them out. One was an inquisitor, who used a spellbook which Pickle took.

Used a combination of magic and brute force to take the large crate full of weapons and load it back onto the barge. was interrupted by the troll again; this time it was given actual gold.

Morkargh was given the ability to fly, to help with the crate. Excited he flew quite far up in the air and encountered a psudodragon which was looking for it's friend - the troll. 

The barge had a cage with some messenger pidgeons, so the party sent a message back to Ronseal asking for help with the "building supplies" on the barge, then took shelter behind some rocks on the shale beach, and lit a fire. Night falls, and Pickle opens the inquisitor's spellbook.

Went to sleep. Morkargh decided to try to search for where the psudodragon which had dived into the sea, and by pure chance found the underground cave where the troll was living.

Angry at the trespass, the troll followed Morkargh up to the surface. The psudodragon explained that the troll was obsessed with the idea of buying a house in the new Monster Sanctuary apparrently run by a unicorn.

The troll was satisfied once it was given more money and left.


## 21st
Set off on the barge to the west, going around the coast . Boots and 2x4 

A message was sent to Folder who replied with surprise and told the party to follow the coast and that he would intercept.

Attacked by orcish pirates. Mork killed the captain, his first officer then challenged mork and lost - putting mork in charge of the ship.

Having no way to actually enforce this, and not wanting to keep a bunch of orcs as pets, the party suggested that the crew join the resistance - the conversation around that idea continues.


Met with sparky

## 25th
Exited the mountains into the elephant graveyard. met an elephant spirit which then returned to the bones which had a rusty old spear point sticking out of them

Got attacked by a patrol of the royal army. Pickle went down temporarily.

Went to the town of Pitts Landing. Organised some upgrades to weaponry and bought some potions. Learned about the history of the town's vendetta against the wild elephant herds.

Met Nigel the elephant (actual name tooth) who could smell the spear point. Was able to get hold of some Larynx Fern to make a potion of speak with animals, and learned that Tooth was captured during the elephant genocide and trained thanks to some tabaxi who convinced the town to spare him.

Nigel decided to break out and go to the elephant graveyard. the party followed, saving him from an ogre and finally explaning to him what had happened.

Nigel then decided he was going to go back to the town and delete the statue.

Klevane's plot was revealed - his plan all along had been to ambush the party. However, he has had a change of heart, given that they literally descended into the underworld to save him.

## 27th of winter
Used Klevane's expected dead drop to send false information back to Dirge. Dirge now believes the party is heading south. Instead, the party have walked in a loop and are heading north again, back toward the dwarven mountains.

Hunted and killed a dire boar which provided much food
