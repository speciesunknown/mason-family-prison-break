# The Elemental Forge

A workshop where magical items are crafted using elemental forces. The forge contains multiple workstations, each connected directly to one of the Elemental Planes, allowing craftsmen to channel raw elemental energy into their creations.

## Layout

The forge is a large circular chamber with four primary workstations arranged around a central anvil:

- **Fire Forge (North)** - Connected to the Plane of Fire, burns with eternal flames
- **Water Forge (East)** - Connected to the Plane of Water, contains a pool of liquid magic
- **Earth Forge (South)** - Connected to the Plane of Earth, features a stone anvil that never cools
- **Air Forge (West)** - Connected to the Plane of Air, uses wind and lightning to shape materials

The central anvil is made of all four elements fused together, allowing for the combination of elemental properties in a single item.

## Current State

**Corrupted Connections:**
- The planar connections have been damaged by yugoloth interference
- Elemental energy leaks unpredictably, causing dangerous surges
- The forges occasionally summon uncontrolled elemental creatures
- Some workstations are unstable and may explode if used incorrectly

**Yugoloth Activity:**
- 2 [mezzoloths](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/17099-mezzoloth) are attempting to forge weapons, but their lack of proper training is causing the corruption
- They've been trying to create weapons infused with infernal energy, mixing elemental and fiendish power
- Their experiments have destabilized the planar connections

## Features

**Elemental Surges:**
- Every 1d4 rounds, roll on the Elemental Surge table
- These surges can be dangerous but also provide opportunities for enhanced crafting

**Crafting Stations:**
- Each forge can be used to craft items with that element's properties
- Requires proficiency with smith's tools or jeweler's tools
- DC varies based on item complexity (typically DC 15-20)
- Success creates a magical item with elemental properties
- Failure may result in elemental backlash or summoning

**The Central Anvil:**
- Can combine multiple elemental properties
- Requires multiple crafters working in harmony
- DC 25+ for complex multi-element items
- Currently unstable due to corruption

## Elemental Surge Table (Roll d6)

| Roll | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 1 | **Fire Surge:** Flames erupt from the Fire Forge. All creatures within 10 feet take 2d6 fire damage (DEX save DC 15 for half). Fire-based crafting gains advantage for 1 minute. |
| 2 | **Water Surge:** The Water Forge floods the area. All creatures within 15 feet must make a STR save DC 15 or be knocked prone. Water-based crafting gains advantage for 1 minute. |
| 3 | **Earth Surge:** The Earth Forge shakes violently. All creatures must make a DEX save DC 15 or fall prone. Earth-based crafting gains advantage for 1 minute. |
| 4 | **Air Surge:** Lightning arcs from the Air Forge. All creatures within 10 feet take 2d6 lightning damage (DEX save DC 15 for half). Air-based crafting gains advantage for 1 minute. |
| 5 | **Elemental Rift:** A random elemental creature is summoned (roll d4: 1=Fire, 2=Water, 3=Earth, 4=Air). The creature is hostile and attacks the nearest non-elemental creature. |
| 6 | **Stabilization:** The forges briefly stabilize. All crafting attempts gain advantage for the next 10 minutes. No surges occur during this time. |

## Potential Crafting Projects

**Fire-Infused Weapon:**
- Requires Fire Forge
- Weapon deals +1d4 fire damage on hit
- DC 18 smith's tools check
- Materials: Masterwork weapon, fire elemental essence

**Water-Blessed Armor:**
- Requires Water Forge
- Armor grants resistance to fire damage
- DC 20 smith's tools check
- Materials: Masterwork armor, water elemental essence

**Earth-Enchanted Shield:**
- Requires Earth Forge
- Shield grants +1 AC and resistance to bludgeoning damage
- DC 19 smith's tools check
- Materials: Masterwork shield, earth elemental essence

**Air-Enhanced Boots:**
- Requires Air Forge
- Boots grant +10 feet movement speed and ability to cast *feather fall* once per day
- DC 17 leatherworker's tools check
- Materials: Masterwork boots, air elemental essence

**Multi-Elemental Item:**
- Requires Central Anvil and all four forges
- Combines properties of multiple elements
- DC 25+ depending on complexity
- Requires multiple crafters and rare materials

## Hazards

**Unstable Connections:**
- Using a corrupted forge has a 20% chance to trigger an elemental surge
- Failed crafting attempts have a 30% chance to summon an elemental creature
- The mezzoloths' presence increases instability

**Environmental Dangers:**
- Extreme temperatures near the Fire Forge
- Slippery surfaces around the Water Forge
- Falling debris from the Earth Forge
- Electrical discharges from the Air Forge

## Restoring the Forge

To fully restore the forge's functionality:
1. Defeat or drive away the yugoloths
2. Repair the planar connections (requires *plane shift* or similar magic)
3. Stabilize each forge individually (DC 20 Arcana check per forge)
4. Perform a ritual at the central anvil to harmonize all four elements

Once restored, the forge can be used safely for high-level magical crafting without the risk of surges or accidental summonings.
