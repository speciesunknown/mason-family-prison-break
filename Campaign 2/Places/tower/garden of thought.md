# The Garden of Thought

An extradimensional garden where ideas grow as physical plants. Concepts can be harvested and used in spell research. Some thoughts are dangerous and must be handled carefully. Accessed through a painting of a garden that becomes real.

## The Garden

The garden is a surreal space where abstract concepts manifest as physical flora. Ideas bloom as flowers, theories grow as trees, and memories hang like fruit. The garden responds to visitors' thoughts, sometimes growing new plants based on what they're thinking about.

**Dangerous Thoughts:**
- Some plants represent dangerous or forbidden knowledge
- Harvesting certain concepts can cause psychic damage or madness
- Ideas that have been "pruned" may grow back twisted and corrupted

**Useful Concepts:**
- Spell research ideas can be harvested and used to develop new magic
- Historical knowledge grows as ancient trees
- Mathematical theorems appear as geometric patterns in the foliage
