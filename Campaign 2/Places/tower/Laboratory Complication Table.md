# Laboratory Complication Table

Roll d20 at end of each turn while in BoomBoom's Laboratory

| Roll | Complication |
|------|--------------|
| 1-2 | **Phasing Out:** One random character begins phasing out of reality. They must make a CON save or become incorporeal for 1 round (can't interact with physical objects, attacks pass through them). |
| 3-4 | **Spell Component Explosion:** Unstable spell components react violently. All characters within 10 feet make a DEX save or take 2d6 force damage. |
| 5-6 | **Temporal Echo:** A character's actions from moments ago repeat in a different location. Roll a d4 to determine which character is affected, then they must make a WIS save or be forced to repeat their last action. |
| 7-8 | **Magical Device Malfunction:** A random magical device activates unexpectedly. Roll d4: 1=summons a random creature, 2=creates a magical barrier, 3=teleports a random character 20 feet, 4=discharges energy dealing 1d8 damage to nearest creature. |
| 9-10 | **Reality Flicker:** The laboratory flickers between existence states. All characters make a CON save or have disadvantage on their next attack roll or ability check. |
| 11-12 | **Spell Contamination:** Magical energy from failed experiments contaminates the area. The next spell cast has a 30% chance to have its effect reversed or altered randomly. |
| 13-14 | **Dimensional Leak:** A small portal to another plane opens briefly. A random creature or object (DM's choice) falls through or is pulled in. All characters within 5 feet make a STR save or be pulled 5 feet toward the portal. |
| 15-16 | **Experimental Spell Backlash:** Residual magic from BoomBoom's experiments discharges. All spellcasters must make a CON save or lose their highest level spell slot. |
| 17-18 | **Reality Stabilization Pulse:** The stabilization device emits a pulse. All characters make a WIS save. On success, they gain advantage on their next saving throw. On failure, they take 1d6 psychic damage. |
| 19 | **Brief Stabilization:** The laboratory briefly stabilizes. All characters gain advantage on their next ability check or saving throw. Reality complications are reduced for 1 minute. |
| 20 | **Slow Motion:** Each character gains one additional action which they must use on their next turn |
