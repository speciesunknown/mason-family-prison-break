# The Controller

big brained troll

half of its head was chopped off, and another head grew on top upside down giving it extra brain power

Controls the upper levels of the prison.

Ronseal has stashed the food supplies the party needs, plus some armor and weapons, in a storeroom but the controller recently had its memory wiped. It no longer knows that it was conspiring with Ronseal

The party will need to break into it's room and sabotage the security doors on the upper level, so the attack from Taldaras is as effective as possible - and as big of a distraction as possible (more to the point)
