Here's a clean spell list for **Father Jack** as a 5th-level cleric (Life or Grave domain vibes, but flavored toward magical affliction removal and tactical defense). These are **mostly utility, control, and radiant**—not nuke spells.

### **Spellcasting (Cleric 5)**  
**Spell Save DC:** 14  
**Spell Attack Bonus:** +6

---

#### **Cantrips (at will)**  
- **Sacred Flame** – Radiant sniper tool  
- **Guidance** – Helps with checks, e.g., calming prisoners  
- **Thaumaturgy** – Voice booming prison priest energy  
- **Spare the Dying** – Keep prisoners alive, grudgingly

---

#### **1st Level (4 slots)**  
- **Command** – *“Stop.”* *“Kneel.”* *“Return.”*  
- **Bless** – Buff allies or “stabilize” prisoners in ritual  
- **Cure Wounds** – Standard healing  
- **Sanctuary** – Protects himself or a non-hostile NPC  

---

#### **2nd Level (3 slots)**  
- **Hold Person** – His go-to “No, you’re not leaving.”  
- **Lesser Restoration** – Cleanses afflictions  
- **Prayer of Healing** – Post-combat ritual healing for prisoners  

---

#### **3rd Level (2 slots)**  
- **Dispel Magic** – Remove curses, enchantments, illusions  
- **Spirit Guardians** *(optional)* – Defensive holy zone if they mob him  
    > Flavor: glowing caducei and radiant chains swirl in a 15 ft aura  

---

If you want to keep it simpler, you can drop **Dispel Magic** and **Spirit Guardians**, and just let him use **Hold Person**, **Lesser Restoration**, and **Command** to keep the players in line.
